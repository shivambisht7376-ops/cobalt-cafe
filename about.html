Our Story

Where It All Began

Precision Brewing,
Electric Ambition

Cobalt Caffe was founded in 2024 with a bold mission: to redefine the urban coffee experience through scientific precision and modern design. Founder Talha Khan spent years studying the chemistry of coffee, obsessed with the perfect extraction and the energy of metropolitan life.

After leading innovative coffee labs across London and Melbourne, Talha brought his vision to the Financial District of New Delhi. He created Cobalt Caffe as a high-performance sanctuary for the modern professional — a place where cutting-edge technology meet artisanal passion.

Our Journey

Milestones

2022

The Blueprint

Talha completes his thesis on molecular extraction and begins designing the 'Cobalt Standard' brewing protocol.

2023

Building the Hub

Acquisition of the Cobalt Plaza site in Delhi's Financial District. Construction of the tech-integrated coffee bar begins.

Jan 2024

Soft Launch

Cobalt Caffe opens for alpha-testing with the city's top tech professionals. The Nitrogen Cold Brew sets a new local record.

Mid 2024

Full Launch & Recognition

Awarded "Most Innovative Workspace" by Delhi Urban Review. Launch of the mobile ordering app.

2025

Growing the Family

Second location in the pipeline. Online coffee subscription launched. 10,000 happy regulars and counting.
